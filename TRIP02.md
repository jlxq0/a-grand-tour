## Driving Conditions

### India
- **Highways:** Improving rapidly (expressways good)
- **Rural roads:** Variable, can be rough
- **Traffic:** Chaotic in cities, horn essential
- **Fuel:** Widely available
- **Tips:**
  - Avoid night driving (animals, trucks without lights)
  - City driving extremely stressful
  - Consider hiring local drivers for city transit

### Nepal
- **Roads:** Generally poor outside Kathmandu
- **Mountain roads:** Narrow, steep, landslide-prone
- **Fuel:** Available in towns
- **Tips:**
  - 4WD essential for most routes
  - Monsoon (Jun-Sep) can close roads
  - Check conditions before mountain sections

### Bangladesh
- **Roads:** Improving main highways
- **Traffic:** Extremely congested
- **Ferries:** May need river crossings
- **Tips:**
  - Very flat terrain (easy driving)
  - Frequent stops at checkpoints
