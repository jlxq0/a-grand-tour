## Practical Notes

**Weather:** Best Oct–Apr for Florida (avoid summer heat/hurricanes). Fall ideal for Blue Ridge (foliage).

**Newfoundland Ferry:** Marine Atlantic runs North Sydney ↔ Port aux Basques. Book in advance for vehicle.

**Blue Ridge Parkway:** No commercial traffic, designed for leisure driving. Many overlooks and hiking trails.

**Florida Keys:** Book Key West accommodation well in advance. Limited supply, high demand.

**Overseas Highway:** Spectacular but can be slow with one lane each direction. Allow time.

**Shipping:** RoRo car shipping from Jacksonville to Southampton takes ~14 days. Book through international shipping agent.
