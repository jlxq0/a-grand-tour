## Key Safety Decisions

### 1. Skip Balochistan (Ship Car)
**Risk:** Active insurgency (Balochistan Liberation Army), kidnapping, recent attacks on foreigners.

**Solution:** Ship car from Bandar Abbas (Iran) to Karachi (Pakistan). Family flies separately (RoRo ships don't carry passengers).

**Cost:** $1,500-2,500 | **Duration:** 3-5 days

### 2. Skip Kashmir (Use Manali-Leh Route)
**Risk:** India-Pakistan conflict zone, UK FCDO advises against travel.

**Solution:** Enter India at Amritsar, drive via Chandigarh to Manali, then Manali-Leh highway through safe Himachal Pradesh. Avoids Srinagar-Leh route through Kashmir entirely.

### 3. Iraq Safety
**Reality:** Southern Iraq (Basra, Ur) is relatively safe. Many overlanders now transit with families. Professional checkpoint system, local hospitality. Main risk is bureaucracy, not violence.

## Iran

### Visa Requirements

**German Citizens:**
- Can travel independently
- Standard tourist visa process
- No escort required

**Canadian/US/UK Citizens:**
- Must have pre-arranged guided tour with government-approved escort
- All accommodations pre-booked in registered hotels
- Cannot travel independently

### Driving in Iran
- **Carnet de Passage** required
- Foreign vehicles permitted up to 3 months
- **Engine size:** The 2500cc restriction was lifted in 2019 for tourists - Land Cruiser 300 (3.3L) is allowed
- **Banned:** US-made vehicles, luxury brands (Porsche, Maserati, Lamborghini) - Toyota is fine
- Fuel extremely cheap (~$0.08/liter)
- Speed limits: 50 km/h city, 70-120 km/h highways
- Aggressive driving culture

### Iran Route (14 days max)

### Karakoram Highway
The world's highest paved international road. Out-and-back trip from Islamabad (can't cross to China).

**Route:**
1. Islamabad â†’ Abbottabad â†’ Besham â†’ Chilas â†’ Gilgit
2. Hunza Valley (Karimabad) - stunning mountain scenery

4. Khunjerab Pass (4,693m) - Pakistan-China border

**Notes:**
- Best months: May-October
- Road can close due to landslides
- KKH is paved but narrow in sections

### Exit to India
- **Wagah Border** (Lahore-Amritsar) - only land crossing
- Famous daily flag-lowering ceremony
- Carnet de Passage accepted
