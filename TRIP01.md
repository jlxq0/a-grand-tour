# Trip 01: Dubai to India Overland

## Route Overview

**UAE → Iran → Pakistan → India**

This is the classic overland route connecting the Middle East to South Asia. Despite perceived dangers, this route is regularly completed by overlanders with proper planning.

---

## Iran

### Visa Requirements

#### German Citizens
- Can travel independently
- No escort/guide required
- Standard tourist visa process

#### Canadian Citizens
- **Must have pre-arranged guided tour** with government-approved escort for entire stay
- Cannot travel independently (due to no diplomatic relations since 2012)
- Book through authorized Iranian travel agency
- All accommodations must be pre-booked in registered hotels
- Visa-free zones (Kish Island, Qeshm Island) available for up to 14 days, but still need escort

### Driving in Iran

- **Carnet de Passage** required for temporary vehicle import
- Can obtain CDP at border or from home country automobile club
- Foreign vehicles permitted for up to 3 months (may be shortened at border)
- **⚠️ Vehicles over 2500cc**: Limited to 5 days only - verify this with tour agency as Land Cruiser 300 (3.3L diesel) exceeds this limit

### Fuel & Roads
- Fuel extremely cheap: ~$0.08 per liter
- Highways generally good quality
- Aggressive driving culture, high accident rates
- Speed limits: 50 km/h city, 70-110 km/h rural, 70-120 km/h highways
- Many speed cameras and speed bumps

### Border Crossings

**Entry from UAE:**
- Ferry from Sharjah to Bandar Abbas (11 hours)

**Exit to Pakistan:**
- **Rimdan/Gabd Border** (recommended) - opened for CDP vehicles April 2025
- Better than old Taftan crossing
- Open 8:30am - 2:00pm
- Iranian side closed Friday, Pakistani side closed Sunday

### Safety
- Generally safe for tourists
- Political tensions with West don't affect tourist safety
- Follow guide's instructions (for Canadian citizens)

## Pakistan

### Balochistan Transit

The province between Iran border and rest of Pakistan has security concerns:
- Separatist activity (Balochistan Liberation Army)
- Kidnapping risk
- Banditry in remote areas

**Solution: Mandatory Security Escort**
- Pakistan provides **FREE mandatory escort** through Balochistan
- Escort arranged at border crossing
- If no personal vehicle, authorities provide transport
- This is standard procedure for all foreign overlanders

### Border Crossing from Iran

**Rimdan/Gabd Border:**
- Opened for vehicles with Carnet de Passage in April 2025
- Several successful crossings by foreigners in campervans
- Immigration and customs takes a few hours
- Authorities still learning CDP process but it works

### Driving Through Pakistan

**Recommended Route:**
1. Cross at Rimdan/Gabd with escort
2. Escort through Balochistan to Quetta or beyond
3. Continue to Lahore via main highways
4. Cross to India at Wagah Border

**Karakoram Highway (if heading north):**
- Avoid Mansehra-Chilas section via Dasu (FCDO advises against)
- Use Babusar Pass alternative (open May-October)
- Main KKH is paved and manageable
- Watch for rockfalls and landslides

### Permits & Requirements
- **No Objection Certificate (NOC)** required for:
  - Balochistan (automatically arranged with escort)
  - Some areas of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa
  - Side valleys off KKH
- Contact local authorities or travel agency in advance
- 4WD vehicle recommended

### Safety Notes
- Main tourist corridor (Islamabad, Lahore) is relatively safe
- Many tourists travel KKH without issues
- Police may be extra cautious during religious events (Ashura)
- Avoid areas near Afghan border

## India Entry

### Wagah Border Crossing
- Only land border crossing between Pakistan and India
- Located between Lahore (Pakistan) and Amritsar (India)
- Famous for daily flag-lowering ceremony
- Carnet de Passage accepted

### Requirements
- Valid Indian visa (obtain in advance)
- Vehicle documentation
- Carnet de Passage

## Alternative Routes Considered

### Via Central Asia (Not Recommended)
**Route:** UAE → Iran → Turkmenistan → Uzbekistan → Tajikistan → China → ...
- **Problem:** China does not allow foreign private vehicles

### Via Iraq/Syria (Not Recommended)
**Route:** UAE → Iraq → Syria → Turkey → ...
- **Problem:** Syria is active conflict zone, extremely dangerous

### Via Afghanistan (Not Recommended)
- Taliban control, extreme danger, no tourist infrastructure

---

## Summary

The **Iran → Pakistan** route is the only viable overland connection between Middle East and South Asia for private vehicles. Despite its reputation, thousands of overlanders have completed this route successfully. Key requirements:

1. **Carnet de Passage** for vehicle
2. **Guided tour for Canadians** in Iran
3. **Accept security escort** through Balochistan
4. **Proper timing** - avoid extreme weather and religious holidays
5. **Flexibility** - conditions can change, stay informed
