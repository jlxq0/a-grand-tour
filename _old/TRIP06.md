## Altitude Considerations

Several sections of this route exceed 4,000m elevation:
- Paso de Jama (Chile/Argentina border): 4,200m
- Salar de Uyuni area: 3,650m
- La Paz: 3,640m
- Lake Titicaca: 3,812m
- Cusco: 3,400m

Allow time for acclimatization when ascending to altitude.
