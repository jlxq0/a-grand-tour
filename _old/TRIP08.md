## Practical Notes

**Weather:** Best Oct‚ÄìApr (dry season). Hurricane season Jun‚ÄìNov on Caribbean coasts.

**Border Crossings:** 7 land borders. Paperwork can take 1-3 hours each. Expect vehicle fumigation fees.

**Safety:** El Salvador and Honduras have reputations but tourist routes are generally safe. Use common sense, don't drive at night.

**Driving:** Mix of excellent highways (Mexico, Costa Rica) and rough roads (Guatemala highlands, Belize). 4WD useful for Semuc Champey access.

**Visas:** CA-4 agreement (Guatemala, Honduras, El Salvador, Nicaragua) = one visa for all four. Panama, Costa Rica, Belize, Mexico separate.
