## Practical Notes

**Weather:** Jul–Oct = Alaska summer into fall. Perfect window. Midnight sun in June/July, aurora possible in September.

**Alaska Highway:** Well-maintained but remote. Carry spare tire, fuel up when possible, be prepared for wildlife on road.

**Border Crossings:** US → Canada at Blaine (WA), Canada → US at Top of the World Highway (Yukon/Alaska).

**Denali:** Book bus/shuttle tours well in advance. Private vehicles limited to first 15 miles.

**Wildlife:** Bear country throughout. Carry bear spray, store food properly.

**Daylight:** Summer in Alaska/Yukon has 20+ hours of daylight. Bring eye mask for sleeping!
