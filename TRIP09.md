## Practical Notes

**Weather:** Best Nov–Apr. Summer very hot on Pacific coast. Copper Canyon pleasant year-round (high altitude).

**Devil's Backbone:** Dramatic mountain road but well-paved. Allow extra time, go slow.

**Copper Canyon:** Remote area. 4WD helpful but not essential on main roads. Chepe train is alternative.

**Baja Ferries:** Topolobampo → La Paz (11.5 hrs, overnight). Book in advance for vehicle. ~$268 USD for SUV + driver.

**Border Crossing:** Tijuana/San Ysidro can have long waits. Cross early morning or late evening. SENTRI lane if you have Global Entry.

**Flights:** LA has excellent connections to Singapore (Singapore Airlines direct LAX–SIN, 17 hrs).
