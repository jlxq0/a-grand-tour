## Practical Notes

**Weather:** Sep–Dec = ideal for Southwest (not too hot). California pleasant year-round. Texas cooling down from summer.

**Route 66:** Historic highway runs parallel to I-40. Look for original alignments and vintage motels/diners.

**National Parks:** Consider America the Beautiful Annual Pass ($80) — pays for itself quickly with this many parks.

**Scenic Routes:** Pacific Coast Highway (CA-1), Route 66, Million Dollar Highway (US-550).

**Altitude:** Colorado/New Mexico mountains reach 10,000+ feet. Allow time to acclimatize.
